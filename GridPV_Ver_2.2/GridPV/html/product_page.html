Version

GridPV Version 2.1

System Requirements

  • GridPV requires OpenDSS 7.6.3 or higher
  • No other MATLAB toolboxes are necessary

Features

  • Standardize interface between MATLAB and OpenDSS for easy parameter queries
  • Model solar power easily and accurately
  • GUI for setting up PV plants
  • Model solar variability for size and dispersion of PV
  • Reactive power control for PV plants
  • Integrate GIS functionality through Google Maps
  • Perform timeseries and steady-state simulations
  • Plotting and visualization of results through many different style plots
